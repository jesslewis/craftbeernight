Beer + Food + Friends = Craft Beer Night

About Us

We are ten friends who love craft beer, good food and great conversations! Combine those and you get Craft Beer Night where we take our love of craft beer and pair it with great food for an awesome night. Each month we pick a different type of beer and plan a menu to compliment the flavors of the beer.


We hope you enjoy what we've put together and encourage you to start your own Craft Beer Night!

Lagers

ABV: 4% - 6%
Examples: Colorado Native Amber Lager, Devil's Backbone Gold Leaf Lager, Brooklyn Lager, Golden Road 329 Days of Sun Lager, Bell’s Lager of the Lakes or Star Hill Jumo Lager

Lager is a type of beer that is conditioned at low temperatures, normally in cold storage at the brewery, before being delivered to the consumer. It may be pale, golden, amber, or dark.

Pilsners

ABV: 3.2% - 5%
Examples: Angel City Gold Line, Dogfish Head Piercing Pils, Oskar Blues Mama’s Little Yella Pils, Lagunitas Pils, Victory Prima Pils or New Belgium Blue Paddle Pilsner

Pilsners originated from Bohemia in the Czech Republic. They are medium to medium full bodied and are characterized by high carbonation and tangy Czech varieties of hops that impart a crisp finish.

Browns

ABV: 4% - 7%
Examples: Peak Organic Nut Brown Ale, Shipyard Brewer’s Brown Ale, Ithaca Nut Brown Ale, Lagunitas Brown Shugga’, Goose Island Nut Brown Ale or Bell’s Best Brown

Browns tend to be maltier and sweeter on the palate, with a fuller body. Color can vary from reddish brown to dark brown. Some will lean towards fruity, while others might be drier with nutty characters.

Stouts

ABV: 4.5% - 8%
Examples: Allagash Black Stout, Left Hand Milk Stout, Founders Imperial Stout, Victory Storm King, Shiners Birthday Beer or Kentucky Bourbon Ale Stout

Stout is a dark beer made using roasted malt or roasted barley, hops, water and yeast. Stouts were traditionally the generic term for the strongest or stoutest porters produced by a brewery.
