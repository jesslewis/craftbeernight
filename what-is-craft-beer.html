What is Craft Beer Night

Craft Beer Night is all about spending a night with friends enjoying craft beers paired with delicious food. We encourage you to come visit our website to find past beer dinners to try with you and your friends.

A typical Craft Beer Night brings us all together after work. We have a no talk about work rule! Where we crack open specific beers and try them with different appetizers, entrees, and desserts. Finding that some pair great and others maybe not as much!
